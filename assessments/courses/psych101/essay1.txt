TITLE: Nature vs. Nurture Essay
TYPE: essay
MIN_WORDS: 500
MAX_WORDS: 1000

---

PROMPT:
The nature versus nurture debate has been central to psychology for over a century. In this essay, take a position on how much of human behavior and personality is determined by genetic factors (nature) versus environmental influences (nurture).

Your essay should:
1. Clearly state your thesis on the nature-nurture question
2. Discuss at least two psychological studies or theories that support your position
3. Address potential counterarguments to your position
4. Consider how the interaction between genes and environment might affect development

Use specific examples and cite relevant research discussed in class or from your readings.

RUBRIC:
- Thesis (20%): Clear, arguable thesis statement that takes a position on the debate
- Evidence (30%): Accurate use of at least two psychological studies or theories
- Analysis (30%): Deep interpretation that goes beyond summary; addresses counterarguments
- Writing (20%): Clear organization, proper grammar, appropriate academic tone
